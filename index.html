About us

We connect, we nurture, we grow

Chess2Mate is a non-profit company founded by CEO Tran Quang Tuan in 2020.

Our goal is to help children under 15 years-old learning and playing chess with healthy altitude. We believe that by letting your children has early contact with high intelligent board games like chess during young age, it could boost their IQ and critical thinking.

We also emphasize connection with other playmates and show them the greatness of fair and healthy sportmanship spirit via offline tournaments.

Stand for connectivity, healthy sportmanship mentality and growth, our brand strive to help more than 500 children in our country not only elevate in IQ but also support their mental health as well as their intellectual skills.

CEO. Tran Quang Tuan
